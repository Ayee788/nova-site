Privacy Policy

Effective: May 13, 2026

Who we are

NOVA Mobile is built and maintained by Andrew Yee as a companion app for the NOVA voice assistant — a Raspberry Pi-based home assistant. Contact: ayee788@gmail.com.

What this app does

NOVA Mobile lets you control a NOVA hardware device from your phone over your home Wi-Fi network. The app is a remote control and companion — it does not function without paired NOVA hardware.

What data we collect

We collect nothing.

NOVA Mobile has no servers, no analytics, no third-party trackers, and no telemetry. The developer has no visibility into how you use the app.

What data the app accesses on your device

The app requests these iOS permissions, all of which are used only to communicate with your own NOVA hardware on your local Wi-Fi network:

Permission Why it's needed Where the data goes
Microphone So you can speak voice commands to NOVA Sent to your NOVA hardware on your home network. Audio is processed locally on your NOVA device and not transmitted off your network.
Contacts So NOVA can call your contacts by name when you say "call Mom" Sent to your NOVA hardware on your home network. Stored on your NOVA device. Never transmitted to the developer or any third party.
Local Network So the app can find your NOVA hardware on your Wi-Fi Stays on your network. No data leaves your network.
Bluetooth Optional — so your NOVA can pair with Bluetooth speakers Stays on your network and device.

What we don't do

NOVA Mobile does not:

• Use advertising or analytics
• Include any third-party SDKs
• Sell or share data with anyone
• Send any data to the developer
• Store anything in the cloud

The app talks only to your own NOVA hardware, which lives on your home network. Your data stays in your home.

Demo Mode

If you enter "demo" as the Pi address during onboarding, the app runs in a hardware-free Demo Mode using canned sample data. Demo Mode does not access the microphone, contacts, or local network — it shows pre-baked example content only.

Children's privacy

NOVA Mobile is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
