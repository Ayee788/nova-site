What NOVA Mobile does

  • Companion app for NOVA hardware — pairs over your home Wi-Fi
  • Talk to NOVA from your phone — voice transcription happens on the Pi
  • See NOVA's holographic face live, mirrored on your phone
  • Control smart home devices: lights, thermostats, locks
  • Launch Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, and other streaming apps on your Roku
  • Daily scripture, devotionals, prayer mode, faith-rooted encouragement
  • No analytics, no third-party trackers, no cloud — data stays in your home

NOVA Mobile is the companion app for the NOVA hardware project.
